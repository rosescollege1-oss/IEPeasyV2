A Message from Chassity

With nearly 15 years in Arkansas classrooms, I’ve seen the "paperwork mountain" grow every year.

My journey in special education has always been about the students. I’ve been recognized for my dedication to their growth, but I realized that teachers can't give their best when they are drowning in manual scoring and complex documentation. I created IEPeasy to give you the exact systems I use to stay organized, compliant, and—most importantly—present for my students.

- Chassity Brown
